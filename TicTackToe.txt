### **Core Gameplay**
1. **3×3 Grid Board** – Classic Tic Tac Toe layout.
2. **Two Players (Local)** – Player vs Player on the same device.
3. **Player Symbols** – X and O (randomly or user-selected first player).
4. **Win Detection** – Horizontal, vertical, diagonal wins.
5. **Draw Detection** – Auto-detect when board is full with no winner.
6. **Move Validation** – Prevent placing on occupied cells.

### **Single-Player Mode**
7. **AI Opponent (Easy/Medium/Hard)**  
   - Easy: Random moves  
   - Medium: Blocks win + random  
   - Hard: Minimax algorithm (unbeatable)

### **UI/UX (Minimalist)**
8. **Clean, Responsive Design** – Works on phones/tablets, portrait mode.
9. **Tap-to-Place** – Intuitive touch controls.
10. **Visual Feedback**  
    - Highlight winning line (animated)  
    - X/O animations on placement  
    - “Win!” / “Draw!” / “Your Turn” messages
11. **Score Tracker** – Wins for Player 1, Player 2, AI, Draws.
12. **New Game Button** – Instant reset.

### **Settings & Customization**
13. **Theme Toggle** – Light/Dark mode.
14. **Sound Effects (Optional)** – Tap sound, win jingle (toggle on/off).
15. **Vibration on Move/Win** – Haptic feedback (optional).
16. **Choose Symbol First** – Let user pick X or O (vs AI).

### **Technical & Performance**
17. **Offline Play** – No internet required.
18. **Fast Load (<1 sec)** – Native or lightweight framework (Flutter/React Native).
19. **Low Battery Usage** – No background processes.
20. **Portrait Lock** – Simple orientation.

### **Nice-to-Have (Still Small)**
21. **Local Stats** – Total games, win %, streak.
22. **Undo Last Move** (1-step, optional).
23. **Language Support** – English 
24. **App Icon & Splash Screen** – Branded, minimal.

### **Monetization (Optional, Non-Intrusive)**
25. **One-Time “Remove Ads” Purchase** (if ads are shown post-game).
